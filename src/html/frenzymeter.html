Frenzy Meter
The frenzy meter starts empty. As you click on blocks, it gets filled up, and maxes out at 50 before resetting.
The frenzy meter activates one of the following skills:
x2 Multiplier
Doubles your score
Duration: 10 seconds
Rarity: 0.800
Touch of Midas
Turns all into gold, doubles score
Duration: 5 seconds
Rarity: 0.195
Purge
Purge everything!!! with x5 score
Duration: instant
Rarity: 0.005
Note: while a skill is running, you cannot activate the meter even if it is full
